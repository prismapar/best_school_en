The Best School in Colombia

Are you looking for a school for your son?

The search engine of the best schools allows parents to find the best learning place for their child, understanding as the best school the one that suits their need and preference among quality, size, positioning, location, among others.
The best school is based on the definition of a ranking with relevant criteria such as: process of students during their academic life, physical facilities such as laboratories, level of inclusion of students, quality of teachers, among others. The search engine has 9,523 private and official schools that correspond to the A and B calendars of all the municipalities in Colombia.

What is the synthetic index of educational quality defined by the Ministry of Education?


The ranking is a value from 1 to 100, which is composed of 3 simple parts:
1. Tests SABER 11: It is the average of the results of the SABER tests of grades 3, 5, 9 and 11.
2. ISCE: It is the sum of the result of the Synthetic Index of Educational Quality for primary, secondary and middle.
3. Level of inclusion: It is the multiplication of the students enrolled in grade 11 who did not present the SABER 11 Tests for the Overall score of the SABER tests 11 taking into account the standard deviation.
The three parts are calculated as a percentage of their maximum, and each of the three parts has the same weight (33.3%) in the total value of the ranking.
